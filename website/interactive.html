Interactive Dark Matter Detection

Upload an image of a galaxy grid, mark regions with dark matter, and let our quantum ML model predict dark matter locations.

How to Use:

  1. Upload Image: Click the upload area or drag and drop a galaxy grid image
  2. Mark Dark Matter: Click on galaxies where you believe dark matter is present (red markers)
  3. Mark Background: Click on galaxies that are background/no dark matter (blue markers)
  4. Generate Grid: Click "Generate Galaxy Grid" to create a synthetic grid from your image
  5. Predict: Click "Run Prediction" to let the quantum ML model predict dark matter locations
